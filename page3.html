List of Winners of the Prestigous "Video Game of the Year" Award from the Past 10 Years


What is the Video Game of the Year award?


The Video Game of the Year award is a trophy and achievement handed to video game that were picked by vote to be the most incredible pieces of art that was released that year. Although there are many ceremonys held to commemorate this event with different winners, I will be using the winners of the D.I.C.E. Awards since it is the most popular.

Fun Facts about Video Games





  1. 2013: The Last of Us

    2. The Last of Us is an Action-Adventure video game praised for its unique concept, engaging gameplay, and incredible storytelling, cementing its place as the 2013 Game of the Year. It was even popular enough to get its own TV show adaptation that was also hailed for its incredibly storytelling!

  2. 2014: Dragon Age: Inquisition

    3. Dragon Age: Inquisition is an Action Role-Playing video game that was praised for its ability to explore the world, its unique combat systems, and its unique and fantasy-themed writing. However, it also received criticism for its plentiful technological issues, its tactical view, and certain aspects of the narrative.

  3. 2015: Fallout 4

    4. Fallout 4 is an Action Role-Playing game set in a nuclear wasteland, with the core aspect being the management and preservation of a "fallout shelter," earning its name. This game was praised for its world depth, player freedom, overall amount of content, crafting, story, characters, and soundtrack.

  4. 2016: Overwatch

    5. Overwatch is a First-Person Shooter, or FPS for short, that was praised for its unique concept, fun gameplay, and elements of teamwork and perserverance. There is currently an Overwatch 2, but it is doing terribly at the time of writing.

  5. 2017: The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

    6. The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild is an Action-Adventure game that was a smashing success and is widely considered one of the greatest video games of all time. This game is absolutely incredible due to its storytelling, gameplay, music, and graphics.

  6. 2018: God of War

    7. God of War is an Action-Adventure game that was hailed as an incredible piece of art by many due to its amazing storytelling, engaging gameplay, riveting musical scores, and much more. This is one of my favorite video games as well!

  7. 2019: Untitled Goose Game

    8. Untitled Goose Game is a Puzzle-Stealth game where you play as a goose looking to cause mayhem. This game was praised for its unique graphics, funny gameplay, and a truly incredible "honk" button.

  8. 2020: Hades

    9. Hades is a rogue-like video game where you play as the son of Hades trying to reach Mount Olympus. This game was praised for its gameplay, replay value, art direction, music, narrative, and voice acting.

  9. 2021: It Takes Two

    10. It Takes Two is an Action-Adventure game praised for its incredibly storytelling, emotional moments, fun and creative gameplay, unique puzzles, and its overall message.

  10. 2022: Elden Ring

    11. The latest winner of the Video Game of the Year award, Elden ring is an Action Role-Playing game in which you travel a decayed land in search of the Elden Ring to become the Elden Lord. This game was praised for its incredibly gameplay, engaging storytelling, creative concept, world-building and musical score, and INCREDIBLY difficult boss fights.
